== Description ==

This plugin is developed by an official PayPal partner and verified by PayPal, providing a secure and reliable payment solution. It offers seamless integration with PayPal, Pay later and credit & debit card, ensuring an easy checkout experience for your customers. Additionally, it supports advanced credit card payments, Google Pay, and Apple Pay, allowing for flexible payment options in your WooCommerce store.

The plugin is fully compatible with popular dynamic discount plugins like Conditional Discounts for WooCommerce, WooCommerce Dynamic Pricing & Discounts, and Discount Rules for WooCommerce.

With clean, efficient coding and a dependable payment process, the plugin enhances your store's checkout system. The PNX and PayPal teams work together to offer fast, professional support to meet your business needs.

This solution is designed to handle multiple payment methods, including PayPal, Pay Later, credit & debit card, advanced credit cards, Google Pay, Apple Pay, and more, ensuring a smooth and flexible payment experience.

It offers a reliable payment process, thanks to its simplified coding and small installation package. At only around 88K, the plugin is very compact and efficient, ensuring that your payment processing runs smoothly and without any issues.

By streamlining the code, it's very to install, configure, and use the plugin. == Installation ==

= Requirements =

To install Advanced PayPal Payments for WooCommerce plugin for your woocommerce store, you need:

* WordPress Version 5.3 or newer (installed)
* WooCommerce Version 3.9 or newer (installed and activated)
* PHP Version 7.1 or newer
* PayPal personal or business account

= Instructions =

1. Log in to WordPress admin.
2. Go to **Plugins > Add New**.
3. Search for the **Advanced PayPal Payments for WooCommerce** plugin.
4. Click on **Install Now** and wait until the plugin is installed successfully.
5. You can activate the plugin immediately by clicking on **Activate** now on the success page. If you want to activate it later, you can do so via **Plugins > Installed Plugins**.

= Setup and Configuration =

Follow the steps below to connect the plugin to your PayPal account:

1. After you have activated the Advanced PayPal Payments for WooCommerce Plugin, go to **WooCommerce  > Settings**.
2. Click the **Payments** tab.
3. Enable **Advanced PayPal Payments for WooCommerce** and click on the **Manage** button.
4. Click on the **CONNECT PAYPAL** tab.
5. Input your PayPal account. If you do not have a PayPal account yet, sign up for a new PayPal account.
6. After you have successfully connected your PayPal account, click on the **PAYMENT OPTIONS** tab to enable PayPal.
7. Click **Save** button.
